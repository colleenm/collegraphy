Ariadne

Ariadne is a different kind of social network, where groups of collaborators post and respond to longform articles and track which information they believe. The project grew out of a year of collaboration and research with Bryce Hidysmith, based on shared concerns about the way current social networks mould discourse into brief spurts of unaccountable and often unsubstantiated dialogue. Other project members include Jack Gallagher, Alex Alekseyenko, and Kat Meler.

Ariadne is currently on hold. Three out of five team members left to found the Median Group.
