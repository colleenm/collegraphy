AI Insights Dataset

The AI Insights dataset is a list of major technical insights in the history of progress in AI, annotated with metadata about the discoverer(s) of each insight. The dataset backs an interactive model calculating the time it would take to reach the cumulation of all AI research, based on a guess at what percentage of AI discoveries have been made.

I compiled this dataset while working at the Median Group, with guidance on insight selection from collaborating researchers there at the time. More details on this project and others available on the Median Group's research page.
